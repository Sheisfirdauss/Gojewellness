Patient Education

Knowledge is an important part of recovery. Learn simple ways to prevent injuries, improve posture and maintain a healthier lifestyle.

Health Information

🩺 What is Physiotherapy?

Physiotherapy is a healthcare profession that helps people restore movement, reduce pain, improve strength and regain independence after illness or injury.

🪑 Good Posture Matters

Maintaining proper posture while sitting, standing and lifting reduces strain on your muscles and joints and helps prevent pain.

🦴 Preventing Back Pain

Exercise regularly, maintain good posture, avoid prolonged sitting and lift heavy objects correctly to protect your spine.

🧠 Stroke Recovery

Early physiotherapy after a stroke can improve balance, coordination, strength and independence during recovery.

🦵 Arthritis Care

Regular movement, weight management and physiotherapy can reduce stiffness, improve joint movement and ease discomfort.

🏃 Preventing Sports Injuries

Warm up properly, use the correct technique, wear suitable footwear and allow adequate time for recovery after exercise.

Healthy Living Tips

🚶 Stay Active

Aim for regular physical activity to keep your muscles, joints and heart healthy.

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink enough water daily to support healthy muscles, joints and overall body function.

😴 Get Enough Rest

Quality sleep allows your body to repair tissues and recover effectively.
