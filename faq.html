Common Questions

Do I need a doctor's referral before seeing a physiotherapist?

No. You can book an appointment directly. However, if you have been referred by a doctor, please bring your referral letter and any relevant medical records.

How long does a physiotherapy session take?

Most sessions last between 30 and 60 minutes, depending on your condition and treatment plan.

How many treatment sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies depending on your condition, its severity and your progress. Your physiotherapist will discuss this after your assessment.

Do you provide home physiotherapy services?

Yes. Home visits are available for patients who are unable to come to the clinic or who prefer treatment in the comfort of their homes.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring any previous medical reports, X-rays, MRI or scan results, referral letters (if any), and wear comfortable clothing that allows easy movement.

Can physiotherapy help with chronic pain?

Yes. Physiotherapy can help manage chronic pain by improving movement, strengthening muscles, reducing stiffness and teaching strategies to prevent future problems.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through the Appointment page, call either of our phone numbers or send us a WhatsApp message.

Where is the clinic located?

GOJE Wellness & Physiotherapy Centre is located in Ilorin, Kwara State, Nigeria.
